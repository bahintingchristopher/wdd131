Site Name: Bahins Web and Software Solutions

I choose this site name because it contains the part of my surname and this contains also the services that we want to offer.

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to offer affordable and professional software and website development services to small businesses and service-based establishments. These include small stores, online shops, salons, massage SPA's, water refilling stations, cooperatives, associations and similar businesses. The site will showcase available services, pricing options, portfolio samples, and a contact form to help small business owners easily start their digital journey.

Scenarios

The website should answer the following scenario questions:

  • What types of software or websites do you build for small businesses?
  • How much does it cost to get a website or software project done?
  • How long does it usually take to complete a project?
  • Do you offer support or updates after the project is finished?
  • Can I see samples of your previous work or client testimonials?
