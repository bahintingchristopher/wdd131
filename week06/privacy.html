Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2025

Introduction

At Bahins Web & Software Solutions, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with our website and services.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, such as when you:

  • Fill out contact or inquiry forms
  • Send us emails or messages
  • Request quotes or project consultations

The types of information we may collect include your name, email address, phone number, company or organization name, and any details relevant to your inquiry or project.

We are the sole custodians of this information and do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

How we use your informations

The data you provide allows us to:

  • Respond effectively to your questions or requests
  • Provide accurate project estimates and service information
  • Send you important updates, offers, or newsletters if you opt-in
  • Improve our website, services, and customer experience

Your information will only be shared internally with team members or trusted service providers who assist us in delivering our solutions, always under strict confidentiality.

You can opt out of promotional communications at any time by contacting us directly.

Data Security

We prioritize the security of your personal information. We use industry-standard measures and controls to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

Only authorized employees or contractors who require access to perform their duties can view your information, and they are bound by confidentiality agreements.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tools to enhance your experience, analyze site traffic, and remember your preferences.

Cookies help us understand how visitors use our site and improve functionality.

You may choose to disable cookies in your browser settings. However, some features of the website may not work as intended without them.

Your Consent and Rights

By using our website and services, you consent to this Privacy Policy. We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in practices or legal requirements, and will post the latest version here with the updated date.

If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy, please contact us at bahintingchristopher@gmail.com.
