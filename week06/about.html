About Us

Bahins Web & Software Solutions is a passionate tech team helping small and local businesses embrace digital tools through websites, custom systems, and smart software. Our mission is to make technology accessible, affordable, and effective for everyone — even without technical knowledge.

Meet the Founder

Christopher Bahinting is the founder and lead developer of Bahins Web & Software Solutions. A graduate of the Computer Programming course in 2004, with a background in web and software development, also studying at BYU Idaho through BYU Pathway Worldwide, and a passion for solving real-world problems with code, he started Bahins to help local businesses modernize without stress.

What We Believe In

  • Simplicity: Solutions should be easy to use and understand.
  • Affordability: We keep costs low without sacrificing quality.
  • Support: We offer personalized assistance every step of the way.
  • Innovation: We're always learning and improving.

Why Choose Us?

We understand the challenges of small businesses — because we're one too. Our goal is to remove the technical barriers so you can focus on what you do best, while we handle the tech.
