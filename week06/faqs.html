Frequently Asked Questions

Why does my business need a website?

A website increases your online visibility, helps attract new customers, and provides information about your products or services 24/7.

Can a website help me build credibility?

Yes, a professional website builds trust with your audience and shows that your business is reliable and established.

How does having a website improve customer communication?

Your website can have contact forms, live chat, and FAQs, making it easy for customers to reach you anytime.

Will a website help me compete with larger businesses?

YES!. A well-designed website levels the playing field, allowing small businesses to showcase their strengths online.

Can a website boost my sales?

Yes! A website can showcase your products/services, provide easy ordering or inquiry options, and attract more customers to increase sales.

What types of websites do you create?

We build custom, responsive websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP tailored to your specific business needs.

How long does it take to finish a project?

Typically, projects take between 2 to 4 weeks to complete, depending on the scope and complexity.

Do you offer custom web applications?

YES! we develop custom PHP-based web applications to automate processes, manage data, and enhance your business operations.

Can you redesign my existing website?

YES! We can update your website's look, improve usability, and make it mobile-friendly.

Do you provide website maintenance services?

YES! we offer ongoing support to update content, fix issues, and keep your website secure.

How do you ensure my website will perform well?

We focus on clean code, fast loading speeds, mobile responsiveness, and SEO best practices to ensure your site performs optimally.
